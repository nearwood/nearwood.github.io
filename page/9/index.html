Ternary computing

I stumbled on an interesting page months ago, and have just gotten back into it. I originally wondered why chips couldn’t be designed in ternary instead of binary logic. It turns out that there was some experimentation in Russia in the 1950s, but not much else. Luckily, someone else [LINK] had this idea and even made an emulator (simulator?) for a ternary chip. He based it off the 6502 8-bit chip (very similar to the one put in Nintendo’s first system), which as it turns out is an excellent, simple design that I can jump into easily.
